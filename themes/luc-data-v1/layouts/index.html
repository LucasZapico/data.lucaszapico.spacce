Presentist

Since February 2019, I took the opportunity to move back into something a bit more analytic and have been a marketing analyst specializing in Microsoft Bing platform. Due to Bing rapid growth over the last five years, I found myself working with processes and tools that were very much still iterating. As such, it was a perfect opportunity to start acquiring the beginning of a data science tool kit. I leveraged a bit of mental muscle developed through my maths and a budding computer science foundation to hop into python, pandas, and matplotlib. The data analyst tool kid did not disappoint.
